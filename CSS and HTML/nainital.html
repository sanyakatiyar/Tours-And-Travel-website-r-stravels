Misty lakes and mountains - NAINITAL

The gem of Uttarakhand
Price : Rs 7,000
Duration : 3 nights 4 days
Tour Lead : Mr. Prateek

Nainital is a charming hill station that sits prettily at the green foothills of the Kumaon ranges in the Himalayas. Located at an elevation of around 1938 metres, the epicentre of the towns popularity and beauty lies within the gorgeous Naini Lake, after which the town is named. It is a perfect weekend getaway from the Indian capital of Delhi.

The recent trip was led by our core Tour Leads - Mr. Prateek. A 4 day trip was absolutely successful with a group of around 40 people joining us to open themselves up to the mountains. Staying in the midst of the city, it is always a great experience. The pleasent stay at the Lake New hotel in itself was a treat for everyone.

Day 1 consisted of a visit to the beautiful lake named as 'Naini Lake' where the travelers witnessed the beauty of the green and blue serene environment.

Day 2 : the travellers went out to look out in the Mall Road to seek for the merchandises as well as souveniers. Which was then followed by a visit ot the Eco Cave Gardens.

Day 3 : A trip to the Snow View point was followed by then visiting the Tiffin top. The view there is extremely beautiful.

Day 4 :Consisted of a visit to the Lake and then leaving for the Delhi.

This quaint little town experiences a pleasant climate throughout the year, making it a tourist getaway, especially preferred by families and honeymooners. The Naini Lake, along with the other lakes in the area, has earned this town the title of 'City of Lakes'.
